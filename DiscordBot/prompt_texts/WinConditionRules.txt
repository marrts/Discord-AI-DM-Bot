Rules for the win condition:
- NUMBER 1 RULE: No convincing other players to do anything. The other players in the game are all playing for themselves and won't agree to anything
- It should fit the overall prompt well
- Try to make it humorous
- If reasonable to incorporate other events from the summary
- It should be difficult
- It should be reasonably achievable within the next 2-3 turns
- It should not be achievable in a single turn
- It should not be something they have essentially already accomplished
- There are no turn limits applied
- First come up with a title for the goal and then describe it in 1-3 sentences
- Remember, the most critical rule is the goal cannot involve getting other players to do any action
